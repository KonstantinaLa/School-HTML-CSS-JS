7 Reasons why you should learn to code

1. Being Able to Turn an Idea Into an App

Knowing how to code, you can take matters in your hand and start bootstrapping one the same day. Save the trouble and build a working prototype yourself. When you know how to code — your imagination is the limit, as you could create anything you dream off.

2. Programming Keeps Your Mind Active and Busy… for Life

The programming world is a fast-paced environment. What worked five years ago might not be the best approach today. Programming languages and frameworks evolve almost daily. If you’re “lucky” enough to carry a hyper-industrial personality, meaning you have to work at least six days out of the week — programming definitely keeps you busy as there’s a never-ending river of information to absorb.

3. Coding Teaches You How to Approach Problem-Solving

Coding is all about seeing the bigger picture which you can break down into smaller particles — those smaller particles being smaller tasks that you can actually finish.

4. Automate Daily Tasks and Save Time

If you’re creative, there’s a chance you woke up one morning with a great idea and fantasized about building the next Apple or Snapchat. You might wonder…What now?

5. Job Security and a Great Salary

Of course, the salaries are great when working in tech.Coders are well paid because the job itself is demanding and difficult. You work around some of the smartest folks on the globe and they expect you to perform.

6. Fun Hobby

Not everyone has to work in a professional environment. Coding is much more than a corporate 9-5 job. Let your imagination run wild and code as many cool projects as your heart desires — no one can stop you. Coding can be a fun hobby for anyone, there are no entry barriers. Anyone who has an internet connection and a computer can start coding as of this moment. Coding adheres to no limitations.

7. Vibrant and Friendly Community

Coders are some of the most fun, adorable, and light-hearted people I’ve met.
